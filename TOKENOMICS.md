## 4. Why No Token Yet â€” Risk Mitigation

Launching a token prematurely introduces significant risks:

### Regulatory Risks
- Securities classification uncertainty
- Jurisdictional compliance complexity
- Potential enforcement actions
- KYC/AML requirements

### Market Risks
- Low liquidity leading to manipulation
- Extreme volatility harming users
- Speculation overshadowing utility
- "Pump and dump" vulnerability

### Project Risks
- Misaligned incentives (trading > building)
- Community fragmentation
- Resource diversion to token management
- "Token-first, product-later" failure pattern

### Our Approach: Build First

```
Phase 0 (NOW)     â†’ Contributors fund â†’ Network grows â†’ App matures
Phase 1 (FUTURE)  â†’ DAO votes â†’ Token created â†’ Distribution begins
Phase 2 (LATER)   â†’ Full decentralization â†’ Community governance
```

This approach ensures:
- Product-market fit before tokenization
- Genuine community of users, not speculators
- Regulatory clarity before token creation
- Sustainable economics based on real utility
